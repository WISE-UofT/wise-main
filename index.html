ABOUT US

WISE is a group of future engineers, scientists, doctors, leaders, programmers, researchers, and more, who all have the shared goal of supporting women in STEM. At our heart, we are a community that believes women should lift each other up and work together.

From running events spanning from Spilling the Tea on Research to Plasmid Bracelet Workshops, we are committed to our initiatives not just because we believe they are necessary, but also because we are passionate about our commitment to each other and to all future women in STEM.

OUR INITIATIVES

To develop women’s leadership skills, confidence, and involvement in STEM fields, WISE focuses on building a supportive community of men and women through 4 initiatives.

Professional Development

Our Professional Development initiative hosts interactive networking events for students throughout the year with companies in software, IT, science, pharmaceuticals and more!

Community Outreach

Our Community Outreach initiative reaches out to the GTA community to share our mission of promoting women's perserverance to achieve gender quality in STEM fields.

Industry Mentorship

Our Industry Mentorship initiative pairs female undergraduate students in science and engineering with passionate mentors who are knowledgeable and experienced in their field. ​

High School Outreach

Our High School Outreach initiative aims to encourage and present diverse opportunities in STEM to high school students through workshops, conferences and mentorship.

Empowering women, 2-spirit, and non-binary individuals in STEM to achieve their full potential as engineers, scientists, and leaders while celebrating their achievements and breaking barriers.
