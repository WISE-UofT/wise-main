WISE International Women's Day Gala: Call for Performers!

Women in Science and Engineering (WISE) is looking for musicians, dancers, and poets to perform at our International Women's Day Gala! The Gala will be held in the evening of Sunday, March 8, 2020 at Hart House in Downtown Toronto. There will be ~100 guests in attendance.

Auditions are open to everyone and all genders and skill levels are encouraged to audition! Submit your video auditions by January 14, 2020!

We encourage but do not require performances with themes of equality, empowerment, and/or inclusivity. Your video auditions should be of the performance you would do at the gala.

Questions or Concerns

Please email Elizabeth at elizabeth.zoubakina@wiseuoft.org if you have any questions or concerns!
