November 23, 2024

Go Eng Girl

​Go ENG Girl is a free annual event for female (cis/trans), non-binary, and two-spirited students in Grades 7 to 10, designed to spark curiosity about the dynamic world of engineering! Through engaging, hands-on activities like building wind turbines and extracting strawberry DNA, students will gain insight into engineering concepts while exploring how this exciting field shapes the world around us. A keynote speaker will also share their journey, offering inspiration and a firsthand perspective on the diverse opportunities in engineering.
