WISE HS Outreach Ambassador 2022-2023 Recruitment

The application deadline has passed. Check back next year!

Women in Science and Engineering is recruiting for HS Outreach Ambassadors for the 2022-2023 year! The primary role of the Outreach Ambassador is to visit high schools across the GTA to present and promote post-secondary opportunities in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics) fields through a presentation and Q&A. You will also have the opportunity to gain authorship in educational material that reaches our audience of more than a thousand students per year. Our overall goal is to encourage high school students to pursue STEM careers by reaching out to different groups in the community.

Apply to become an Outreach Ambassador for the 2022-2023 year!
