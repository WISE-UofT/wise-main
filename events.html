March 23rd, 2024

Go Code Girl

Computer science isn't just a subject; it's a mindset—an approach to problem-solving and creativity. From healthcare to finance, entertainment to transportation, computer science is quickly becoming the backbone of innovation and advancement in our everyday lives. Discover why more women and non-binary youth are choosing computer science for a challenging and impactful career. Go Code Girl offers students in grades 7-10 hands-on coding activities led by women undergraduates, covering topics like loops, binary systems, and AI.
